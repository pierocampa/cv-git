Relevant Funded Research Projects
=================================

TRANSALP: Transboundary Storm Risk and Impact Assessment in Alpine regions.
January 2021 - December 2022, supported by European Commission’s Directorate
General European Civil Protection and Humanitarian Aid Operations (DG ECHO)
Union Civil Protection Mechanism (UCPM) research and innovation programme
under grant agreement n.101004843. Website: https://project-transalp.eu/

BURUNDI IOM: Multi-Hazard Assessment and Risk Mapping in Burundi.
September 2020 - September 2021, financed by the International Organization
of Migration (IOM). 
https://www.eurac.edu/en/institutes-centers/institute-for-earth-observation/projects/burundi-iom

EARTHSERVER: European Scalable Earth Science Service Environment.
September 2011 - August 2014, funded from the European Community’s
Seventh Framework Programme (EU FP7) under grant agreement n.283610
(https://cordis.europa.eu/project/id/283610).
Website: https://earthserver.eu/
